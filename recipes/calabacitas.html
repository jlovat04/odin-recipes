Calabacitas

Description

Calabacitas is a savory vegetarian dish. It is eaten in many places. Where I am from in New Mexico it is eaten year round but the best time is in the Summer when Zuchini and Squash start to produce. As well as corn.

It is usually made with fresh ingredients as this is where it gets most of its flavor from. Fresh Squash/zuchini, corn, tomatoes, and onions.

Ingredients

  • Squah or Zuchini
  • Corn
  • Half onion
  • Green Chile or your pepper of choice
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
    •

    Ingredients

    1. Chop all vegetables to your preferred size
    2. Heat pan with a tbsp of oil
    3. Cook onions until translucent
    4. Cook Squash/Zuchini until softened
    5. Put in Chile and Corn and cook for a couple minutes
    6. Put in tomatoes and cook for another couple minutes
    7. Season to your liking
