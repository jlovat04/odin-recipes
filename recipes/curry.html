Japanese Curry

Description

Japanese Curry is a semi sweet savory spicy meal. It can be made with an assortment of meats and vegitables.

The base of the curry is in the roux. This is the most important part of the recipe. The entire dish revolves around this. So it is key that it is done correctly. The sweetness and spice is dependent upon the person making it.

You can use different kinds of meats but the most popular are beef, chicken, and pork. And vegetables include onions, carrots, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • beef,chicken,pork
  • Roux
  • stock/broth of choice
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • onions

    • Steps

    1. Chop vegetables
    2. Cook onions
    3. brown meat
    4. put in carrots and broth
    5. simmer until meat is cooked
    6. Pour in roux and stir until thick
    7. Let simmer for a few minutes
    8. Add sweet component honey/ketchup
    9. Serve over rice
